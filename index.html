Q&A

How do I start my first quest?

Gameplay

To start your first quest, navigate to the Quests section and select "The Lost Artifact" which is perfect for beginners. Click "Start Quest" and follow the guided instructions.

What rewards can I earn from quests?

Quests

Quests reward you with experience points (XP), in-game currency, and special items. Higher difficulty quests offer better rewards but require more skill and time to complete.
